Oran Longmuir and Saya Yamaguchi
Oran and Saya are Edinburgh based circus artists. They have been working together as an acrobatic and aerial duo since 2017 and have performed at a wide variety of events including arts festivals, pride events, cabarets, live music gigs, film projects and corporate functions. Oran (he/they) has a dance background while Saya (she/her) began as an aerialist. Oran's passion is lifting Saya and Saya's passion is climbing on Oran.
